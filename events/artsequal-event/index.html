ArtsEqual Event
June 12, 2018 - June 12, 2018

Venue: Edge Hill University

The Festival of Ideas will be running at Edge Hill University between May and June. The festival is a series of talks, seminars, exhibitions and performances that will explore the prism of equalities and will incorporate the 100 anniversary of women.

As part of this, ArtsEqual (held on the 13th June from 9am-9pm) will see the celebration of the contribution of the Arts and Arts Psychotherapies to Health, Wellbeing and Equality. Our performance group will be performing Getting out of your own way as part of the event and will engage in a Q&A. Our clinical group will be delivering a workshop similar to the workshop that has been proposed to be delivered as part of the Dancing the Blues project. This is a great opportunity to gain an understanding into both phases of the project and allows an insight in to the clinical side of the work. For more information have a look at our blog page where you can see some of the feedback from the session.
