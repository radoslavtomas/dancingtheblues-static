US Book Launch for the Oxford Handbook of Dance and Wellbeing
March 25, 2018 - March 25, 2018

Venue: New York University

After the successful UK launch of the Oxford Handbook of Dance and Wellbeing at Edge Hill University, we are now looking forward to the launch in the US at New York University!! It is our pleasure to invite you to the US book launch on Wednesday 28th March 2018, 3-5pm Pless Hall (First Floor Lounge), 82 Washington Square East, New York University (enter from Washington Square Place). Free and open to the public.

If you happen to be in New York by the end of March, please do join us for this event. For more details:
