World Mental Health Day
October 10, 2018 - October 10, 2018

Venue: Edge Hill University

This World Mental Health Day at Edge Hill University, the Dancing the Blues team will be presenting an outline of their work and further testing the pilot workshop between 12.30pm - 3pm in room H243 in the Facuty of Health and Social Care.


Across the university there is a host of activites which will see mental health organisations across the University and the wider community join together to raise awareness about mental illness and its effects on people’s lives, promote community outreach and provide public education regarding mental health and wellbeing. Activities during the day include art events, educational sessions provided by healthcare professionals, advertising campaigns, mental health fairs and movie nights.
